Introduction

Every church carries stories passed from one generation to the next. Some become treasured memories. Others quietly become traditions that are accepted without ever being examined.

This book is an invitation to slow down, return to Scripture, appreciate those who came before us, and ask honest questions with humility, education, and compassion.

Rather than attacking faith, these pages celebrate the courage to continue learning while recognizing the sacred worth of every person created in the image of God.
